#### **Day 1: Ethics - Need for Ethical Behavior in Computing**
**Objective:** Understand the importance of ethical behavior in computing.
- **Lecture (25 minutes):** Overview of ethics in computing, including privacy, security, and the impact of technology on society.
- **Activity (20 minutes):** Group discussion on real-world ethical dilemmas in computing.
- **Homework (35 minutes):** Research and write a report on a recent ethical issue in the tech industry.
- **Flipped Learning:** Assign a video or reading material on ethical behavior in computing.

#### **Day 2: Copyright Legislation and Software Licensing**
**Objective:** Understand copyright legislation and types of software licensing.
- **Lecture (25 minutes):** Explanation of copyright laws, the importance of protecting intellectual property, and different types of software licenses (e.g., open source, proprietary).
- **Activity (20 minutes):** Group activity to identify and discuss different types of software licenses and their implications.
- **Homework (35 minutes):** Write a short essay on the importance of copyright legislation and software licensing.
- **Flipped Learning:** Provide online tutorials or resources on copyright laws and software licensing.

#### **Day 3: Ownership - Implications and Applications of AI**
**Objective:** Understand the implications and applications of AI.
- **Lecture (25 minutes):** Overview of AI, its applications, and ethical implications.
- **Activity (20 minutes):** Group discussion on the ethical implications of AI in various industries.
- **Homework (35 minutes):** Research and document a case study on the ethical implications of AI in a specific industry.
- **Flipped Learning:** Assign a video tutorial on AI and its ethical implications.

#### **Day 4: Review and Discussion**
**Objective:** Review the week's lessons and discuss key takeaways.
- **Discussion (20 minutes):** Review the key points from the week's lessons.
- **Activity (20 minutes):** Group presentation on the ethical implications of AI and copyright laws.
- **Homework (35 minutes):** Prepare for a quiz on ethics and ownership in computing.
- **Flipped Learning:** Provide additional resources for students to review.
